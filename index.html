About World Radio Atlas

Your portal to global sound, connecting you to over 60,000+ live radio stations across every continent.

What is World Radio Atlas?

World Radio Atlas is a premium, high-fidelity interactive radio directory designed for audiophiles and global citizens. We aggregate live FM, AM, and online radio streams from over 200 countries, providing a seamless listening experience through a state-of-the-art interactive map and a lightning-fast search engine.

How to Listen to Live Radio?

Our platform uses advanced Web Audio API technology to bring you crystal-clear streams. Simply browse the interactive map, click on any city marker, or use our "Deep Search" tool to find your favorite stations by name, genre (Jazz, Rock, Pop, News), or location. Enhance your audio with our built-in styles like Bass Boosted and Spatial Atmos simulation.

Is the Service Free?

Yes, World Radio Atlas is 100% free and community-driven. We utilize the open-source Radio Browser API database to ensure our directory is constantly updated with the latest active streams. Our mission is to preserve the cultural heritage of global radio and make it accessible to everyone, anywhere.

Technical Excellence

Built with performance in mind, our site uses IndexedDB for instant local caching and lazy loading for heavy assets. This ensures that while we maintain a directory of 60,000+ stations, your initial load time remains under 2 seconds, providing an elite Search Experience (SXO).

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I listen to radio on mobile?
Absolutely. World Radio Atlas is fully responsive and optimized for mobile browsers and tablets. You can also install it as a Progressive Web App (PWA) for an app-like experience, streaming your favorite international stations on the go, anywhere and anytime — for free.
What audio styles and EQ presets are available?
We offer five curated EQ presets powered by the Web Audio API: Normal, Bass Boosted, Treble (Tone), Soft, and Spatial (Atmos simulation). You can switch between them live while any station is streaming, tailoring the sound to your headphones or speakers.
How many stations are available on World Radio Atlas?
Our directory currently indexes over 60,000 active live radio stations from 200+ countries globally, from local community FM stations to major international broadcasters like BBC World Service, NRJ, Deutsche Welle, and RTHK.
